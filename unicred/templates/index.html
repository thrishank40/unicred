Platform Features

Everything you need in one place

UniCred brings together resource sharing, knowledge exchange, and trust-building

Resource Sharing

Share electronics, books, sports gear, and more with your campus community. Earn credits for every successful lend.

Knowledge Hub

Offer academic help and skill sessions. Teach what you know, learn what you need — all within the credit economy.

Secure QR Exchange

Physical exchanges are verified through encrypted QR codes with SHA-256 hash signatures and timestamp validation.

Credit Protection

Security deposits are locked automatically. Penalties are applied for overdue returns. Fraud detection keeps the system fair.

Leaderboard & Badges

Compete on the campus leaderboard. Earn badges for milestones and build your reputation as a trusted community member.

Trust Score System

Every transaction is rated on communication, timeliness, and item condition. Your trust score defines your campus standing.
